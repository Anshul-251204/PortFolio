About ME !

I'm Anshul Choure, and my academic journey began in the serene town of Bhoura, Betul district, Madhya Pradesh. I passed my 10th-grade exams with determination and pursued PCM (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics) in the 12th grade, setting the stage for a tech-focused future.The turning point came when I ventured to the bustling city of Indore for higher studies at Sage University. Here, I embarked on a B.Tech journey with a fervor for web development. Through intensive coding, project development, and internships, I honed my skills in front-end and back-end development.As I near the end of my B.Tech program, I reflect on my transformative journey. It's taken me from a small town to a thriving city, equipping me with the knowledge and passion to excel as a web developer. With eyes fixed firmly on the digital horizon, I'm ready to make my mark in the tech world.
